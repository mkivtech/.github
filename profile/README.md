# MkIV Pty Ltd

MkIV is an Australian engineering and technology company delivering capability across Defence, ICT & Software, Space, and Rail. We operate in complex, high-stakes environments where technical rigour and deep domain expertise are non-negotiable.

## What we do

**Defence**
MkIV provides engineering and technical services across the full Defence capability life cycle — from early concept and requirements through to integration, certification, and sustainment. We support programmes of all scales across land, air, maritime, and joint domains, offering systems engineering, requirements management, RFT support, integration design, system safety, and technical certification.

**ICT & Software**
MkIV designs and delivers technology solutions across the full stack and the full discipline spectrum — from AI/ML engineering, data engineering, and cybersecurity through to cloud architecture, DevOps, platform engineering, software development, prompt engineering and AI integration, and UX design. We build bespoke software, design secure enterprise architectures, and embed AI capability into products and workflows. Whether the requirement is a greenfield platform, a legacy modernisation, or a complex AI-driven system, we have the depth to deliver it.

**Space**
In-house rocket system science and engineering capability, with expertise in RF system design, EMC/EMI, RADHAZ assessments, embedded systems, robotics, computational electromagnetic modelling, laser safety, antenna design, and radar systems.

**Rail**
Systems modelling, rail systems design and construction, signal sighting, dwell time requirements, and resolution of RFIs across overhead lines, structures, track, train control, civil, power, operations, and maintenance.
